About

I am a second year Master-Doctor program student in AIGS at UNIST. Currentrly, I am a graduate student at Language & Intelligence Lab, advised by Prof. Hyounghun Kim. I am interested in researching human-like conversational models with long-term memory, multimodal learning, and embodied agents in complex scenarios similar to those in the real-world. Previously, I was a research assistant at Handong Artificial Intelligence Lab (HAIL), advised by Prof. Charmgil Hong.
